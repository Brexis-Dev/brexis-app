Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026 · Saturday Morning PJs · Calvert County, Maryland

Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to Saturday Morning PJs and its operational platform, Purple Horizon ("we," "our," or "us"), a small business based in Calvert County, Maryland. Purple Horizon powers our storefronts, inventory management, and third-party integrations including Pinterest and Etsy.

What information we collect

Through our Pinterest integration, Purple Horizon may access and read the following:

  • Pinterest account information associated with the Saturday Morning PJs business account
  • Pin and board performance data (impressions, saves, clicks)
  • Audience analytics provided by Pinterest to business accounts
  • Content published to our own Pinterest boards

We do not collect, store, or process personal information belonging to Pinterest users who interact with our content.

How we use information

Information accessed through the Pinterest API is used solely to:

  • Monitor the performance of Saturday Morning PJs content on Pinterest
  • Manage and sync content between Purple Horizon and our Pinterest business account
  • Inform internal business decisions about product and content direction

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade any data accessed through the Pinterest API with third parties.

Data storage and security

Purple Horizon is hosted on Railway, a secure cloud deployment platform. All data accessed through third-party integrations is processed within our platform and is not stored beyond what is operationally necessary. We do not retain Pinterest user data. Access credentials are stored securely using environment variables and are never hardcoded or shared.

Third-party services

Our integrations connect to third-party platforms including Pinterest and Etsy. Each of these platforms maintains their own privacy policies which govern how they handle data. We encourage you to review them:

  • Pinterest Privacy Policy
  • Etsy Privacy Policy

Your rights

As a small business operating in Maryland, we comply with applicable U.S. privacy laws. If you have questions about what data we access or how it is used, you can contact us directly using the information below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of our integrations after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
